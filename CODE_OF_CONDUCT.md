# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful and collaborative environment for everyone contributing to the Static Website Generator (SWG) project.

We encourage constructive discussions, knowledge sharing and professional collaboration.

---

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Provide helpful feedback.
- Respect different opinions and experiences.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Offensive language.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or abusive communication.

---

## Project Philosophy

The SWG project values:

- Simplicity
- Maintainability
- Consistency
- Accessibility
- Open collaboration

Technical discussions should focus on improving the project rather than individuals.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience unacceptable behavior or have concerns about interactions within the project, please contact the project maintainers.

All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, reject or remove contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from the project.

---

Thank you for helping make Static Website Generator an open, welcoming and professional project.
